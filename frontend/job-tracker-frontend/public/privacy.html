Privacy Policy

This application, Job Tracker, accesses and processes Gmail data strictly for the purpose of helping users track and organize job application-related emails.

Data Usage

We access your Gmail metadata (such as sender, subject, and body of relevant emails) only with your explicit permission via Google's OAuth system. This data is used solely to identify and categorize job-related emails for your personal use. We do not use your data for advertising or share it with third parties.

Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All access tokens and email metadata are stored securely and encrypted at rest. Access to this data is restricted and monitored to prevent unauthorized access. All communication with our servers is secured using HTTPS.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only as long as your account is active and you have granted us access. You may revoke access at any time via your Google Account permissions page. Upon revoking access or deleting your account, your data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

This app complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

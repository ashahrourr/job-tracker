Privacy Policy

This application accesses and processes Gmail data strictly for the purpose of helping users track job application-related emails.

Here's how we handle your data:

  • We access your email metadata (such as sender, subject, and body) only with your explicit permission via Google's OAuth system.
  • No email content is shared with third parties.
  • All tokens and metadata are securely stored and encrypted in our database.
  • You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account permissions.

This app complies with Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
